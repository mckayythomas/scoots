Tours and Places to Visit

Mayan Bee Sanctuary

Explore ancient Mayan culture as you tour the small cenote and Mayan sculptures located here.

Chankanaab Adventure Beach Park

Snorkel with fish and explore the amazing marine life that makes up the oceans of Cozumel.

Mr Sanchos Beach Club

Relax on the beach while the staff of Mr Sanchos' beach club make your experience unforgettable.

Paradise Beach

Find Paradise your-self in the warm waves and relaxing in the sun of Cozumel.

Palancar Reef

Learn to scuba dive and take in the amazing marine life of the Palancar Reef

Punta Sur Eco Beach Park

Learn how the locals of Cozumel do their best to preserve the natural beauty of the island while enjoying a relaxing day on the beach.

Playa El Cielo

Enjoy the stunning view and waters named after the many sea stars that live in the crystal clear water.

Dolphin Discovery Cozumel

Discover what makes dolphins so exciting to learn about and take a once in a life time opportunity to swim in the same waters.
