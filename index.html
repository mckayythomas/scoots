Welcome to Scoots!

Tired of the touristy feel of traveling? Are you looking to experience the natural beauty of Cozamel with out the stress? Want to explore the Island from the view of a local?

You're in the Right Place!!

Welcome to to Scoots. Cozumel's number one scooter and tour vehicle service. Get ready to see the amazing sights of Cozumel and experience the island how a local would every day. Take in the natural beauty of the coast and the towns as you drive through them on one of Scoots’ highly maintained rentals!

Locations

Puerta Maya

Our location in Puerta Maya allows for easy access for cruise ships. All of our services are offered at this location, but the most popular are our jeep rentals, which allow you to also visit the town as well as explore the island.

Playa Del Carmen

Our location in Playa Del Carmen offers easy access to those who have just flown in to visit the island. Close to the Plaza de Armas makes this location central and perfect for anyone staying in the center of Cozumel.
